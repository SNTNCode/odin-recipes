Moscow Mule

Description

A delightful summer drink, citrusy and sweet.

    Materials

  • Copper Mug
  • Tittos Vodka
  • Lime
  • Lemon
  • High Quality Ginger-Ale
  • Crushed Ice or Small Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Cut half a lemon and a lime and squeeze the juice into the copper mug.
  2. Add 3-4 oz of Tittos to the copper mug
  3. Fill the cup with crushed ice
  4. Top with Ginger Ale
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel or wedge
  6. Gently stir

Enjoy!
