Old Fashioned

Description

A delicious, classic cocktail with hints of orange and a slight sweetness

Getting Started

    Materials

  • Old Fashioned Glass
  • Large Ice Cube
  • Maker's Mark Bourbon
  • Sugar or Simple Syrup
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Maraschino Cherry (Optional)
  • Orange Peel

Steps

  1. Start by adding 3 dashes of Angostura Bitters to the glass.
  2. Then add either 1 sugar cube, or a bar spoon of simple syrup. If it was sugar, muddle with bitters.
  3. Add 3oz of Makers Mark to the glass.
  4. Add a large ice cube and gently stir.
  5. Top with Orange Peel

Enjoy!
