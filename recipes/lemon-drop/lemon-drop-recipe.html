Lemon Drop

Description

A delicious lemony cocktail, smooth and sweet.

Getting Started

    Materials

  • Martini Glass
  • Lemon
  • Honey
  • Cointreau
  • Tittos Vodka
  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Strainer

Steps

  1. Start by adding a whole lemon's juice and honey to the cocktail shaker and stir until the honey is disolved.
  2. Add 1 part Cointreau to 2 parts Tittos into the shaker.
  3. Add Ice to shaker and shake the liquid until it is cold.
  4. Use the strainer and pour the cocktail mix into a chilled martini glass.
  5. Garnish with a lemon wheel.

Enjoy!
